    Turkish Al-Sheikh Honored International Stars at the Entertainment Industry Forum

    The Entertainment Industry Forum started on October 13 at the Riyadh Hotel in the presence of the Chairman of the Turkish Al-Sheikh General Entertainment Authority. The opening ceremony of the conference was attended by a large number of Saudi ministers and officials, and also prominent movie stars from all around the globe.

    The beginning of the Joy Forum was a discussion session called “Entertainment and Building the Economy.” During this session, GEA Chairman Turki Al-Sheikh, Minister of Economy and Planning Mohammed bin Mazid Al-Tuwaijri and Egyptian Minister of Culture Inas Abdel Dayem spoke.

    Entertainment in Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, diversification of the economy and economic transformation, development of the entertainment sector, enhancing people’s lives and improving the quality of life was the focus of the discussion during the session.

    The launch of this forum is intended to enhance the Kingdom’s presence on the entertainment industry map of the region.

    In addition, the launch of the Forum is in line with the Kingdom’s Vision 2030, which seeks to grow the Kingdom’s entertainment industry, making it a global hub for mega-entertainment activities and a significant base for businesses and talent affiliated with the entertainment business. This is in addition to allowing the Kingdom to serve as an attractive investment destination by creating strong alliances to create, expand and invest in all related platforms, particularly infrastructure and utilities.

    A number of stars from all over the world were honored by Al-Sheik in recognition of their huge efforts in this profession. The awardees included Indian superstar Shah Rukh Khan, Chinese star Jackie Chan, American star Jean-Claude Van Dam, Saudi star Nasser al-Qasabi, Kuwaiti star Suad Abdullah, Saudi mega star Mohammed Abdo and Egyptian stars Hani Shaker, Yousra, Raga el-Geddawi, and Issaad Younis.
